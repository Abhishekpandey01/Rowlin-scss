Why you need Smart Heating

Traditional room thermostats can only be controlled when you are at home. A Smart Thermostat provides you with full mobile control, accessing from any Wi-Fi enabled remote device. Efficient and user friendly, you are in full control of your home heating – all of the time. Get A Quote

Control with your phone

We will fit a component directly to your boiler and this will connect wirelessly to the in-home device that is the thermostat and main control . From the Smart Heating app on your mobile device, you can remotely control the thermostat/main control device from wherever you may be.

Your Smart Thermostat can also ‘learn’ your schedule, detecting when your smartphone is in the house or even knowing when you’re on your way home, switching on the heating for when you arrive.
